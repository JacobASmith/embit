Project Team Journal Deliverables

User Stories

User Story 1

Work In Progress

As an electricity consumer and mobile device user, in order to get savings on my energy bill, I can track and report my smartphone energy usage to Embit.

User Story 2

Work In Progress

As an electricity consumer and mobile device user, in order to see my energy bill savings and energy consumption, I can view a dashboard with usage metrics.

User Story 3

Work In Progress

As a logged out user with an account, in order to view the dashboard and report my data, I can type my email address and password to log in to the app.

User Story 4

Work In Progress

As a new user, in order to view my dashboard and report my energy consumption data, I can register with an email address, password, etc.

User Story 5

Work In Progress

As a logged in user, in order to logout and stop reporting data, I can click a logout button.

User Story 6

Work In Progress

As a logged in user, in order to capture and send my data, I can open the app then let it run in the background.

User Story 7

Work In Progress

As the product owner, in order to share green energy consumption of my users with energy companies, I can use aggregate data collected from the smartphones of those users.
